In this task, you will be prompted with a long word (for example: EXPERIMENT). You have to find words contained in this long word. So, create new words with the letters: E X P E R I M E N T.
For example: 'item' is a possible solution to the word 'EXPERIMENT'. Using this letters: E X P E R I M E N T.

Rules of the game:
  • Words have to be at least 4 letters long.
  • Only English words count.
  • Each letter can only be used once per word.
  • Proper nouns do not count (e.g. Sarah, London, Microsoft).
  • Duplicates don't count.
  • Any character that is not A through Z is discarded.
  • Words may be entered in upper, lower or mixed case.
  • You can submit words by pressing ENTER or click the submit button.
A word of 4 letters is worth 2 points, a word of 5 letters is 3 points etc. Your final score on this task will be transferred to euros and added to your potential payoff.

In total you have 2 minutes to write down as many words as you can.
The timer will only show in the last 10 seconds.

By clicking the next button, you confirm to understand the rules of the game.

After clicking the next button, the task and timer will start immediately.
